# Quietly Design Direction

Theme: light, soft daylight, warm white surfaces, sage green accent, small traces of tea and mist blue.

Physical scene: a student or knowledge worker uses Quietly on a phone at a desk near a window, in a library or shared office, wanting calm without feeling like they opened a meditation app.

Typography: system sans, native mobile feel, strong but not oversized headings, compact labels.

Layout: mobile-first, 430px maximum app shell on desktop, WeChat mini-program feeling with clear app boundary and rounded outer shell.

Motion: one signature hero motion explaining noisy sound becoming a smoother masking field. Other motion should be short, calm, and state-driven. Respect reduced motion.

Illustration: soft window, daylight, desk, plant, cup, waveform, and masking labels. Prefer code-native CSS illustration for MVP, with room to replace by a generated image later.
