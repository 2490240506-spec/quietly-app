# Quietly

register: product

Quietly is a mobile-first AI sound masking tool for frequent focus use in offices, libraries, study rooms, shared workspaces, and dorm rooms.

Users open it quickly, allow local microphone analysis when possible, and get a recommended masking sound scene. The product should feel calm, trustworthy, therapeutic, and useful, closer to a polished mini-program than a marketing page.

Core promise: analyze ambient sound locally, identify the dominant noise type at a coarse level, and recommend a stable masking scene. Do not claim true active noise cancellation or complete noise removal.

Tone: warm, precise, quiet, reassuring.

Anti-references: generic white-noise player, cold SaaS dashboard, dark AI tool, childish wellness app, loud glassmorphism, template landing page.
